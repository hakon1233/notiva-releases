# Competitors

One file per competitor. Short enough to read in under a minute, detailed enough to act on.

## File naming

`<competitor-name>.md` — lowercase, hyphens. Use the product name, not the company (Notion > NotionLabs).

## Suggested structure

```markdown
# <Competitor Name>

- **URL:** https://…
- **Pricing model:** free / freemium / per-seat / tiered
- **Target audience:** who they serve
- **Last reviewed:** YYYY-MM-DD

## What they do well
- Strength 1 — specific, observable
- Strength 2

## What they do poorly
- Weakness 1
- Weakness 2

## Features worth stealing
- Feature → why it'd help us, what it'd cost to build

## Features we do better
- Where we win and why

## Screenshots / links
- Link to archive.org snapshot, screenshot in repo, or a Loom
```

## When to add a competitor

- They show up in user interviews more than twice.
- They raised funding / launched something relevant.
- You're about to make a product decision that depends on knowing their stance.
