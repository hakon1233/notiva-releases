# Users

What users actually say, think, and do. The raw material that informs product decisions.

## What goes here

- **Interview notes** — one file per interview: `YYYY-MM-DD-<name-or-handle>.md`
- **Feedback patterns** — recurring themes pulled from support tickets, social media, reviews
- **Personas** — if the project has distinct user types, document each
- **Support ticket analysis** — monthly or quarterly summaries of what broke, what confused users, what they asked for

## Interview note template

```markdown
# Interview: <name or handle> — YYYY-MM-DD

- **Role:** what they do
- **Context:** how we found them, medium (video / in-person / async)

## Background
<paragraph — who are they, what do they care about>

## Problem statements (their words)
- "quote 1"
- "quote 2"

## Workflows they use today
- How they currently solve the problem

## Reactions to our product
- What they liked
- What confused them
- What they'd want

## Takeaways
- 2-3 action items we're taking from this
```

## Rules

- **Anonymize when needed.** Don't put real names in public repos without permission.
- **Quote don't paraphrase** for strong signals. Paraphrase is fine for the summary.
- **Link to recordings** if you have them (Loom, internal drive) — don't embed raw audio.
